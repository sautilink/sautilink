Frequently Asked Questions

What is SautiLink? +
SautiLink is a privacy-first social networking platform built in Tanzania. We prioritize user privacy, freedom of expression, and data sovereignty.
When will SautiLink launch? +
We are currently in the final development stage. Join our waitlist on the homepage to be among the first users when we launch.
How does SautiLink protect my privacy? +
We follow a strict "Privacy by Design" approach. We collect minimal data, do not sell user information, and implement strong encryption standards. Your data belongs to you.
Is SautiLink only for Tanzanians? +
No. While we are proudly built in Tanzania and headquartered in Mwanza, SautiLink is designed for users across East Africa and eventually the entire world.
What is the connection between SautiLink and SautiNote? +
SautiLink Corporation owns and operates SautiNote (@sautinote), our digital media platform focused on independent news and commentary. You can visit www.sautinote.com.
How can I contact support? +
You can reach our support team at support@sautilink.com or use the contact form on our Contact page.

Still have questions?

Our team is ready to assist you. Reach out and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
