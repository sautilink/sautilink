Help Center & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about SautiLink, our waitlist, and our commitment to privacy.

What is SautiLink? +
SautiLink is a new privacy-first social network being built from Tanzania. Our mission is to create a platform where users have full control over their data and can express themselves freely without surveillance.
When will SautiLink launch? +
We are currently in the pre-launch phase. By joining the waitlist, you'll be among the first to know when we officially launch. We're working hard to bring you a high-quality, privacy-focused experience.
How does SautiLink protect my privacy? +
Privacy is at the core of everything we do. We follow a minimal data collection policy, use strong encryption where applicable, and are committed to never selling or sharing your personal data with third parties.
What information do you collect from the waitlist? +
We only collect your email address and desired username when you join the waitlist. This information is used solely to notify you about the launch and important updates.
Will SautiLink be free to use? +
Yes, the core platform will be free. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but basic usage will always remain free with strong privacy protections.
How can I contact the team? +
You can reach us at support@sautilink.com for support or info@sautilink.com for general inquiries. Our headquarters are located on Uhuru Street in Mwanza, Tanzania.
