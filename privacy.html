Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At SautiLink, privacy is not a feature — it is a foundational principle. We designed this platform to give users maximum control over their data and conversations while enabling genuine free expression.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email address and username during waitlist or account creation
  • Content you post (texts, images, videos, links)
  • Messages and interactions with other users
  • Profile information you choose to add

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and device information (for security and abuse prevention)
  • Usage data such as pages visited and features used
  • Browser and operating system type

We deliberately collect minimal personal data compared to traditional social networks.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the SautiLink service
  • Enable you to communicate with others on the platform
  • Protect the safety and security of our users and the platform
  • Respond to legal requests and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Communicate important updates (very rarely)

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Ever.

We may share information only in these limited cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, and property of SautiLink, our users, or the public
  • With service providers who assist us (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored with industry-standard security measures. We use encryption in transit and at rest where appropriate.

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Because we are a privacy-first platform, we avoid unnecessary data collection and long-term profiling.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have significant control over your data:

  • Access, download, or delete your account and content
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Object to certain processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sautilink.com.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to operate the service (authentication, security, basic functionality). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

SautiLink is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Material changes will be communicated through the platform or via email. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
