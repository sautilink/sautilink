Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

At SautiLink, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and giving you full control over your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email address and username when you join our waitlist.
  • Technical information such as IP address, device type, operating system, browser version, and timestamps.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve website functionality and user experience.

When the full SautiLink app and platform launch, we will collect additional information necessary to provide the service, such as profile information, posts, and interactions — all under strict privacy controls.

2. Purpose of Data Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To manage the waitlist and send launch notifications
  • Authentication and account management (when the app launches)
  • Service improvement, personalization, and feature development
  • Security, fraud prevention, and abuse protection
  • Internal analytics to understand how our website and services are used

3. Third-Party Services

We use Cloudflare for hosting, content delivery, and security. We may also use analytics tools to better understand user behavior. These third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only with trusted service providers who help us operate the platform, or when legally required to do so.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@sautilink.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the “Last updated” date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@sautilink.com
