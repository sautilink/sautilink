We believe in a free and private internet.

SautiLink is building the next generation of social networking — one that puts people first.

Founded in Tanzania, SautiLink is a privacy-first social platform dedicated to protecting freedom of expression and user data sovereignty in East Africa and beyond.

In a world where major social platforms increasingly compromise user privacy and censor voices, we created SautiLink as a secure, transparent, and truly user-owned alternative. Our mission is to provide a digital space where Africans — and people everywhere — can connect, share ideas, and express themselves without fear of surveillance or arbitrary moderation.

Our Principles

Privacy by Design

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. End-to-end encryption and minimal data retention are core to our architecture.

Freedom of Speech

We defend the right to free expression while maintaining a respectful environment. We reject political censorship and algorithmic manipulation.

Built in Africa

Headquartered in Mwanza, Tanzania, we understand the realities and aspirations of East Africa. We are building technology that serves our people first.

SautiLink Corporation

SautiLink Corporation is headquartered in Uhuru Street, Mwanza, Tanzania. While our legal base is in Tanzania, we operate with a fully remote-first team to protect the privacy and safety of our builders and users.

We are currently focused on East Africa with plans for rapid expansion across the region. Our goal is to create a sovereign African social infrastructure that prioritizes digital rights.

SautiLink also owns and operates SautiNote — a digital media platform delivering independent news and commentary. Follow @sautinote on Instagram, X (Twitter), Facebook, and YouTube, or visit www.sautinote.com.
