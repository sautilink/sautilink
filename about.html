About SautiLink

We're building a privacy-first social network from Tanzania — a platform where your voice truly belongs to you.

Our Mission

In a world where big tech companies harvest your data and control what you see, SautiLink was born from the belief that people deserve better. We are creating a social network that prioritizes privacy, freedom of expression, and user sovereignty.

Why We Exist

Most social media platforms today profit by selling your attention and personal data. SautiLink is different. We believe your conversations, your photos, and your thoughts should remain yours — not commodities to be tracked, profiled, or monetized.

Our Core Values

Privacy First

We collect the absolute minimum data necessary and never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Freedom of Expression

We defend your right to speak freely while maintaining a respectful and safe environment for all users.

Built in Africa

Proudly developed from Tanzania, we understand the unique needs and aspirations of African users and the Global South.
